# PlayDays vision

## Current MVP
Answer one high-frequency parent question better than alternatives: “What should we do today?”

## Near-term product direction
- Make the daily plan genuinely trustworthy
- Make discovery results feel real and local
- Make the returning-user experience feel progressively smarter
- Turn the app into a dependable morning ritual (home screen + digest)

## What not to build yet
- Broad social/community layers
- Heavy account management complexity before the core daily-planning loop is sticky
- Feature sprawl that weakens the main “today plan” experience

## Success signals
- A parent can go from landing to first useful plan in a few minutes
- Returning users get to value even faster
- The generated plan feels doable, not generic
- The homepage clearly positions the product against obvious alternatives: search, Pinterest/blog posts, local-events scavenging, and ad hoc parenting chats
